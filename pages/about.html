INSTRUMENT

Banjo

The banjo is a stringed musical instrument characterized by a circular body with a thin membrane, usually made of plastic or animal skin, stretched over a frame.

Drum

A drum is a musical instrument that belongs to the percussion family. It consists of a hollow cylinder or shell covered with a stretched membrane, known as the drumhead

Piano

The piano is a musical instrument that produces sound by pressing keys on a keyboard. Each key controls a small felt-covered hammer that strikes a string.

Guitar

The guitar is a musical instrument with strings that are typically plucked or strummed to produce sound.
